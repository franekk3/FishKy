Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 09, 2026

Fisq is built as a free educational tool. Your privacy is a priority, which is why the app is designed to be as transparent as possible regarding your data.

1. Data Collection and Transmission

Fisq does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data to external servers. All your learning data stays where it belongs: on your device.

  • Local Storage: Your flashcards, decks, and progress are saved locally on your phone.
  • Internet Usage: The app uses an internet connection only to download ready-to-use flashcard sets from the official server. No private data is sent back during this process.

2. Permissions

To provide its core features, the app requires specific permissions:

  • Storage Access (Import/Export): Required only when you choose to export your decks to a file or import decks shared by others. The app does not scan your other private files.

3. Third-Party Services

There are no tracking scripts, analytics (like Google Analytics), or advertisement IDs embedded in Fisq. Your activity within the app is completely private.
